## Executive Summary

This project analyzes **OLA ride-booking data for July 2024** to evaluate:

- Operational performance  
- Revenue generation  
- Customer behavior  
- Cancellation patterns  
- Driver efficiency  

The dataset includes **103,000+ ride bookings** generating approximately **₹35 Million in revenue**.

Key finding: While 62% of rides are successful, a significant 38% cancellation/incomplete rate impacts operational efficiency and revenue.

---

##  Business Objective

The objective of this analysis is to:

- Measure booking success and cancellation impact  
- Identify revenue contribution by vehicle types  
- Evaluate ride distance variations  
- Analyze payment trends  
- Assess customer & driver satisfaction  

---

##  Dataset Overview

- Total Bookings: 103K  
- Total Revenue: ₹35M  
- Successful Rides: 63.97K (~62%)  
- Cancelled / Incomplete Rides: 39.06K (~38%)  
- Time Period: July 2024  

---

##  Assumptions

- Dataset accurately represents July 2024 bookings  
- Booking status categories are correctly recorded  
- Ride distance and booking value contain no major errors  
- Ratings are recorded on a consistent numerical scale  
- Vehicle types are properly categorized  
- Payment methods are accurately captured  

---

##  Key Analysis Areas

### 1️ Overall Performance

- Daily bookings: ~3,200–3,450  
- Daily revenue: ₹1.7M–₹1.95M  
- Demand stable throughout the month  

 Insight: No extreme volatility — business operations are consistent.

---

### 2️ Revenue Analysis

- Cash: ₹19.3M (Highest)  
- UPI: ₹14.2M (Second Highest)  
- Cards: Minimal contribution  
- Prime Sedan generates highest revenue (~₹5.2M)  

 Insight: Revenue distribution across vehicle types is balanced.

---

### 3️ Cancellation Analysis

- Successful: ~64K  
- Cancelled by Driver: ~18K  
- Cancelled by Customer: ~10K  
- Driver Not Found: ~10K  

 Insight:
- Driver cancellations exceed customer cancellations.  
- “Driver Not Found” indicates operational gap.  

---

### 4️ Operational Comparison

- Premium vehicles handle longer trips  
- Auto mostly used for short distances  
- Revenue evenly distributed across vehicle categories  

---

### 5️ Ratings Analysis

**Customer Ratings**
- Average around 4.0  
- Prime Plus slightly highest (4.01)  

**Driver Ratings**
- Range: 3.98–4.01  
- Very minimal variation  

 Insight: Service quality is stable across categories.

---

##  Hypotheses Testing

### 1️ Do Successful Rides Contribute Significantly More to Revenue?

- H0: Successful rides do not significantly impact revenue  
- H1: Successful rides significantly impact revenue  

 Result: H0 Rejected  
Successful rides contribute majority of total revenue.

---

### 2️ Does Average Ride Distance Differ by Vehicle Type?

- H0: Average ride distance same across vehicle types  
- H1: Average ride distance varies across vehicle types  

 Result: H0 Rejected  
Premium vehicles handle longer trips.

---

### 3️ Are Customer Cancellations Significantly Impacting Total Bookings?

- H0: Customer cancellations are not significant  
- H1: Customer cancellations significantly impact bookings  

 Result: H0 Rejected  
Cancellations significantly affect operational efficiency.

---

##  Key Conclusions

1. Cancellation rate (~38%) is operationally significant.  
2. Premium vehicles generate higher revenue.  
3. Revenue and bookings show stable daily performance.  
4. Cash is the dominant payment method.  
5. Ratings indicate consistent service quality.  

---

##  Business Recommendations

1. Improve driver availability tracking and reduce driver cancellations.  
2. Introduce driver performance incentives.  
3. Promote premium vehicles during peak hours.  
4. Encourage digital payments via cashback or reward programs.  
5. Launch loyalty programs or ride subscription plans.
